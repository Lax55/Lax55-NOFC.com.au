Bringing Nepal's Spirit to Australian Football

Founded in 2021, the Nepalese Orange Football Club represents the growing Nepalese community in Orange, NSW. Our club was formed to create a sporting home for Nepalese immigrants and their families, while promoting cultural exchange through the world's most popular sport.

We compete in local leagues and organize community events that bridge cultures and bring people together through football. Our vision is to develop players of all ages while maintaining strong connections to our Nepalese heritage.
